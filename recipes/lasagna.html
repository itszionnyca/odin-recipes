Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground turkey
  • 1 jar pasta sauce (24 oz)
  • 9 lasagna noodles (regular or over-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg (optional, mixes with ricotta)
  • Salt, pepper garlic powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Meat
    2.
    1. In a pan, brown 1 pound ground beef
    2. Drain the fat
    3. Add the jar of pasta sauce and simmer for 5 minutes
  2. Cook the Noodles (Skip if Over-Ready)
    3.
    1. Boil water
    2. Cook lasagna Noodles until Soft
    3. Drain and set aside
  3. Mix the Cheese Filling
    4.
    • In a bowl mix:
      • 1 cup ricotta
      • 1/2 cup Parmesan
      • A pinch of salt & pepper
  4. Build the Lasagna
    5.  In a baking dish:
    1. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom
    2. Add a layer of noodles (3 noodles)
    3. Add cheese mixture
    4. Add meat sauce
    5. Sprinkle mozzarella
    6. Repeat the layers two more times
    7. Finish with mozzarella on top
  5. Bake
    6.
    • Cover with foil (so cheese doesn't burn)
    • Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes
    • Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes
    • Let cool 10 minutes before cutting

Done! Enjoy your lasagna
