Cereal

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Cereal
  • Bananas
  • Spoon

Instructions

    Have all ingredients on a flat surface.
    1. Open a box of cereal of your choice.

    2. Pour cereal into empty bowl. (Stop when you see fit)
      3. Note: Do NOT fill more then 3/4ths of the bowl.

    3. Cut up at least half a banana and place gently in the bowl.
      4. (You can replace with any fruit of your choice if preferred.)

    4. Pour milk of your choice until cereal starts rising.

ENJOY!
